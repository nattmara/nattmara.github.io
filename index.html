Trassel Privacy Policy

Photo library access

Photo library access is strictly on-device and solely used to add images into the app.

Support inquiries

Information shared in support inquiries (email addresses and email contents) is retained until the inquiry is resolved.

Other data and information

Beyond the above, Trassel does not access, collect, or transmit any data or information.
