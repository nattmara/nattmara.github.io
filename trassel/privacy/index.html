Trassel Privacy Policy

Photo library access

Photo library access is optional, strictly on-device, retractable, and solely used to add images into the app.

Support inquiries

Information shared in support inquiries, including email address, is retained until the inquiry is resolved.

Other data and information

Beyond the above, Trassel does not access, collect, or transmit any data or information.

Privacy policy questions

For questions regarding this privacy policy, contact developer@nattmara.com
